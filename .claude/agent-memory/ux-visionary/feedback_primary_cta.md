On the Today screen, the new-record CTA should NOT be duplicated as a large button at the top of the screen. The raised `+` in the tab bar is the app-wide grammar for "start an entry," and the numbered ladder's row 1 ("Open new record / Resume draft") is the screen-local grammar.

**Why:** duplicating a primary CTA on the same screen splits attention. In glove/harness conditions, even a half-second of "which one do I tap" costs more than the redundancy is worth. The doc-system aesthetic also relies on the ladder being THE primary affordance for the screen — adding a competing button dilutes the form metaphor.

**How to apply:**
- Today screen keeps ladder row 1 as primary, with yellow emphasis when applicable.
- The tab-bar `+` remains the app-wide entry point.
- This stance is Today-specific. Other screens (Log, Gear) may legitimately have their own header-bar `+ ADD` because the tab-bar `+` is non-contextual there.
- If a future variant of Today removes the ladder entirely, revisit this — the rule depends on the ladder being present and high-contrast.
